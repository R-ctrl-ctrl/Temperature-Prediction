Weather is an important aspect of a person’s life as it can help us to know when it’ll rain and when it’ll be sunny. Weather forecasting is the attempt by meteorologists to predict the weather conditions at some future time and the weather conditions that may be expected. Here, the parameters temperature, pressure, humidity, wind speed, precipitation, pressure is only considered for experimental analysis. Firstly, the data is trained. For training the data, we will take 15-20% of the data from the data set. For this prediction, we’ll be using Linear regression algorithm and Random Forest algorithm. For the project, we have used python, NumPy, Jupiter Notebook, pandas,Flask,Sklearn.
